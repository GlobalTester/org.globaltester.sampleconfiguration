Create a SampleConfiguration

To add a new SampleConfiguration to your workspace, you have to do the following:
Move the mouse cursor into the "GlobalTester Navigator" window. Right-click, and choose "New -> Other...". In the "New" wizard, select "GlobalTester -> SampleConfiguration", and click "Next >". Enter a name for your SampleConfiguration in the text field "File name". Then click "Next >" Now you can enter the configuration of your test sample as you wish. After that click "Finish".
